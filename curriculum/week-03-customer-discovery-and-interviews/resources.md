## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Customer discovery** | Talking to people who have the problem to learn the truth |
| **Get out of the building** | Facts about customers live outside; opinions live inside |
| **Past-behavior question** | Asking what actually happened, not what someone might do |
| **Compliment trap** | Polite praise that feels like validation but is worthless |
| **Synthesis** | Turning many interviews into a pattern |
| **Pivot** | Re-aiming the idea toward the real pain you found |
