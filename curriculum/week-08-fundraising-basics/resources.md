## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Bootstrapping** | Funding from savings/revenue; maximum control |
| **Angel** | An individual early investor |
| **Pre-seed** | First institutional-ish money toward early traction |
| **Cap table** | Record of who owns what |
| **Pre-/post-money** | Company value before/after new money |
| **SAFE** | Money now for shares later, at a future priced round |
| **Valuation cap / discount** | Knobs deciding how much early money is worth at conversion |
| **Option pool** | Shares reserved for future hires; dilutes founders |
| **Dilution** | Ownership % falling as new shares are issued |
