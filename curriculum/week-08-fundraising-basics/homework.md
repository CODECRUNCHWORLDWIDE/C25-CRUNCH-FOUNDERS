# Week 8 Homework

Three problems, about **2 hours**. Work in your Week 8 repo. Push toward ten interviews.

---

## Problem 1 — Raise-or-not memo

**Problem statement.** At `notes/raise-or-not.md`, decide whether your venture should raise now. Cover: is capital the bottleneck, is the model validated, what would the money buy, and how the decision reflects your Week 1 control-vs-wealth lean.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; all four points addressed; an explicit decision tied to your lean. Committed.

**Hint.** "Not yet" is a valid, common, respectable answer.

**Estimated time.** 30 min.

---

## Problem 2 — Round + option pool model

**Problem statement.** At `notes/round-model.md` (with a spreadsheet), model a hypothetical pre-seed: starting cap table, a 10% option pool created pre-money, and an investor ending at 10%. Show founder ownership before and after.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; option pool modeled pre-money; founder dilution shown; tables sum to 100%. Committed.

**Hint.** Watch how the two 10% slices remove roughly 20% of founder ownership in one round.

**Estimated time.** 50 min.

---

## Problem 3 — SAFE in plain English

**Problem statement.** At `notes/safe-explainer.md`, explain in your own words (150–250 words) what a SAFE is, what a valuation cap and a discount do, and why a SAFE dilutes you later.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; 150–250 words; cap and discount both explained; the later-dilution point made. Original prose. Committed.

**Hint.** Read the YC SAFE primer in resources, then write it as if explaining to a co-founder.

**Estimated time.** 30 min.
