## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Business model canvas** | One-page hypothesis of how the business works |
| **Contribution margin** | Price minus variable cost per unit |
| **CAC** | Average cost to acquire one paying customer |
| **LTV** | Total contribution a customer gives before churning |
| **LTV:CAC** | How much a customer is worth vs. cost to acquire |
| **Payback period** | Time for a customer's contribution to repay their CAC |
| **Sensitivity check** | Re-running the model under worse assumptions |
