# Week 1 — Exercises

Short, focused drills. Each one should take 30–60 minutes. Do them in order — the second references the first.

## Index

1. **[Exercise 1 — Founder brief](exercise-01-founder-brief.md)** — write the one-page brief that names your unfair advantages, your control-vs-wealth lean, and a problem space you have standing in. (~45 min)
2. **[Exercise 2 — Control vs. wealth](exercise-02-control-vs-wealth.md)** — place yourself on the control-vs-wealth line and defend it against three concrete future decisions. (~30 min)

## How to work the exercises

- Read the prompt. Skim, don't memorize.
- **Write the answer yourself.** Do not paste a template and lightly edit; type it.
- Use the C25 voice — problem-first, evidence-over-adjectives, honest about trade-offs. No "disruptor" / "10x" / "rockstar" language.
- Commit each completed exercise to your Week 1 repo as you go. Small commits.
- If you get stuck for more than 10 minutes, re-read Lecture 1 or 2.

These exercises do not have a single right answer. They are deliverables you will reference at every later decision. Take them seriously.
