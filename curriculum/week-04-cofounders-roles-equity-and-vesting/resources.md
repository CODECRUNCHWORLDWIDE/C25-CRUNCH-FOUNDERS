## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Co-founder** | A partner sharing ownership and the hardest early decisions |
| **Decision rights** | Who has the final call on a given decision |
| **Equity split** | How ownership is divided among founders |
| **Vesting** | Earning equity over time rather than owning it all at once |
| **Cliff** | A period (often one year) before any equity vests |
| **Acceleration** | Equity vesting faster on an acquisition or termination |
| **Cap table** | The record of who owns what |
