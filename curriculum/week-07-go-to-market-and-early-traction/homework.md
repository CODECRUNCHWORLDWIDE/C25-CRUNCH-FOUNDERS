# Week 7 Homework

Three problems plus a channel test. Work in your Week 7 repo. Keep interviewing toward ten.

---

## Problem 1 — Motion fit memo

**Problem statement.** At `notes/motion-fit.md`, argue why your chosen motion fits your price, customer, and sales complexity — and name one motion you are deliberately *not* using and why.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; fit argued across price/customer/complexity; one rejected motion with reason. Committed.

**Hint.** Reference your Week 6 price. A cheap product cannot afford an expensive motion.

**Estimated time.** 30 min.

---

## Problem 2 — Channel test

**Problem statement.** Run one small, time-boxed channel test (e.g., contact 20–40 prospects, post in a community, publish one piece). Log it at `notes/channel-test.md`: the action, the time box, the pre-set success bar, and the funnel numbers you got.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; action + time box + pre-set bar; funnel numbers recorded. Committed.

**Hint.** Do things that don't scale. Manual outreach is fine and expected.

**Estimated time.** ~2.5 hours (the week's field work).

---

## Problem 3 — Funnel read

**Problem statement.** At `notes/funnel-read.md`, analyze the channel-test funnel: where is the biggest leak, what does it suggest, and what is the next experiment?

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; leakiest step named; a next experiment proposed. Committed.

**Hint.** A bad result is still data. A leak at activation often means the problem/solution fit is still off.

**Estimated time.** 30 min.
