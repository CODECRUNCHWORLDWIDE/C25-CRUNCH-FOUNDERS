## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **GTM motion** | The repeatable way customers come to you |
| **Founder-led sales** | Selling person-to-person, unscalably, early on |
| **One metric that matters** | The single number that best shows progress now |
| **Vanity metric** | A flattering, unactionable number |
| **Funnel** | Reach → interest → activation → retention/revenue |
| **Leakiest step** | The biggest drop in the funnel — fix it first |
| **Retention** | Whether users come back; the truest early signal |
