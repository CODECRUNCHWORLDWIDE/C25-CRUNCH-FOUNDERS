# Exercise 3 — GTM One-Pager Canvas

**Goal:** Fill in a single-page go-to-market canvas that names your motion, your first channel, your One Metric That Matters, your funnel, your leak, and your next move. This canvas is the **spine of your mini-project** — finish it here, polish it there.

**Estimated time:** 40 minutes.

**Format:** A canvas — a structured fill-in template you complete in one page. Copy the canvas below into `exercises/exercise-03-gtm-one-pager-canvas.md` and fill every box. Not code; this is your go-to-market on a single page, the way a lean canvas put your model on a single page in Week 6.

---

## Why a one-pager

A go-to-market plan that takes ten pages is a plan nobody reads, including you. The discipline of one page forces you to know your motion cold: if you can't say how customers will reliably arrive and how you'll know it's working in one page, you don't know it yet. Investors, advisors, and co-founders will all ask a version of "how do you get customers?" — this canvas is your answer, in a form you can defend in 90 seconds.

The canvas pulls together everything from this week: the motion (Lecture 1), the OMTM and funnel (Lecture 2), the test you designed (Exercise 2), and the leak you found (Exercise 1).

---

## How to fill it

- **Be specific.** "Founder-led sales to 4,000 named clinics" beats "B2B sales." Names, numbers, dates.
- **Use real numbers** from your Exercise 2 test where you have them; clearly-labeled estimates where you don't.
- **One motion, one channel, one metric.** The canvas has no room for hedging, on purpose.
- **End with a decision.** The last box is your next move — double down, fix the leak, or pivot the channel — not a vague "keep going."

## Maria's filled canvas (reference)

> **GTM One-Pager — ClinicFlow.** One line: "We cut no-shows ~30% for small independent clinics with automated patient reminders."
>
> **1. Customer + market:** solo / 2-doctor independent dental + medical clinics in TX. ~4,000 serviceable. Nameable? **Yes** (board directories). Price/ACV: **$99/mo = $1,188.**
>
> **2. Motion:** founder-led sales. Fits because the list is small + nameable and the ACV affords a personal demo. Unscalable thing first: I set up the reminder flow by hand for the first five clinics (concierge onboarding).
>
> **3. First channel:** personal cold email to 40 named clinics, 10-day box. Pre-registered bar: ≥ 2 paid OR ≥ 4 trials.
>
> **4. OMTM:** stage = early revenue. OMTM = **clinics that complete a paid onboarding.** Vanity ignored: page views, post likes, "interested" replies.
>
> **5. Funnel:** Reach 40 → Interest 12 (30%) → Activation/demo 6 (50%) → Trial 3 (50%) → Revenue 2 (67%). **Leakiest step: demo → trial.**
>
> **6. Economics:** blended CAC ≈ $500 (20 hrs @ $50). LTV $1,400. LTV:CAC ≈ 2.8:1. Payback ≈ 7 months.
>
> **7. Next move:** **fix the leak and re-run.** Specifically: rebuild the demo around each clinic's real no-show numbers and add same-day trial setup; re-run cold email on 40 fresh clinics, then scale to 200 if the demo→trial rate clears 60%.
