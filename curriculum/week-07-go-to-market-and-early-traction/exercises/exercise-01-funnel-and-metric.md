# Exercise 1 — Funnel and Metric

**Goal:** Build your acquisition funnel, choose the single metric that matters, and identify the leakiest step.

**Estimated time:** 45 minutes.

---

## What to produce

Create `exercises/exercise-01-funnel-and-metric.md`:

```markdown
# Funnel & metric — <your idea>

## Chosen motion
(Founder-led / community / content / paid / partnerships — and why it fits.)

## My one metric that matters
(The single number; why this stage cares about it.)

## Three vanity metrics I will ignore
- ...

## Funnel (with numbers — real or honest estimates)
Reach: ___
Interest: ___
Activation: ___
Retention/Revenue: ___

## Leakiest step
(The biggest % drop, and what I would try to fix it.)
```

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] File present; motion chosen with reasoning.
- [ ] One metric named for the stage.
- [ ] Three vanity metrics explicitly set aside.
- [ ] Funnel with a number on each step.
- [ ] Leakiest step identified with a fix idea.
- [ ] Committed.

---

## Hints

- If your motion is paid ads but your audience is tiny and your price low, reconsider — the motion likely does not fit.
- Retention is the truest signal; if you can estimate it, do.
