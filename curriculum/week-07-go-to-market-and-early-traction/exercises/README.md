# Week 7 — Exercises

Three focused exercises that build directly toward this week's mini-project (the GTM one-pager + traction test). Do them in order — each one feeds the next.

- **Exercise 1** is a guided walkthrough: you build your funnel, pick your one metric, and find the leak. Worked end-to-end with Maria's ClinicFlow as the example.
- **Exercise 2** is a worksheet/template: you design the time-boxed channel experiment you'll actually run Wednesday–Thursday, with a pre-registered success bar.
- **Exercise 3** is a canvas: the fill-in GTM one-pager that becomes the spine of your mini-project deliverable.

These are not coding exercises. C25 is a founders course — you'll write target lists, outreach copy, funnel numbers, and decision rules. The "build" is your go-to-market, not software.

2. **[Exercise 2 — Channel test plan](exercise-02-channel-test-plan.md)** — a worksheet/template for designing a cheap, time-boxed channel experiment with a pre-registered success bar. (~40 min) — template.
3. **[Exercise 3 — GTM one-pager canvas](exercise-03-gtm-one-pager-canvas.md)** — a fill-in canvas for the go-to-market one-pager. (~40 min) — canvas.

## How to work the exercises

- **One motion, one metric.** Pick the single best-fit motion (Lecture 1) and the single OMTM for your stage (Lecture 2). Don't hedge with three.
- **Put real numbers on every funnel step.** Use real data where you have it; honest estimates clearly labeled `(est.)` where you don't. A funnel with no numbers is a wish, not a funnel.
- **Ignore vanity metrics on purpose.** Name them explicitly so you can't sneak them back in.
- **Pre-register every success bar.** Decide what "a win" means *before* you see results. Write it down with a date.
- **Be honest.** These are for you. A flattering funnel you can't defend helps no one — least of all the future-you reading it before demo day.
- **Commit each to your Week 7 repo** under `exercises/` so each produces an artifact you can point to.
