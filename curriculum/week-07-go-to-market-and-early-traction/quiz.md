# Week 7 — Quiz

Five questions. Notes closed. Answer key at the bottom.

---

**Q1.** A go-to-market "motion" is:

- A) A pitch deck slide.
- B) The repeatable way customers come to you.
- C) The product roadmap.
- D) A funding round.

**Q2.** Founder-led sales is the best fit when:

- A) The product is cheap with a massive audience.
- B) The product is higher-priced with few, reachable customers.
- C) You have a huge ad budget.
- D) There is no product yet.

**Q3.** Which is a **vanity** metric for an early startup?

- A) Conversion to paid.
- B) Weekly retained users.
- C) Total page views with no usage behind them.
- D) Recurring revenue.

**Q4.** In a funnel, you focus first on:

- A) The step that is easiest to improve.
- B) The leakiest step — the biggest percentage drop.
- C) The top of the funnel only.
- D) Every step equally.

**Q5.** The truest early signal of product value is:

- A) Social media followers.
- B) Total signups.
- C) Retention — whether people come back.
- D) Press coverage.

---

## Answer key

<details>
<summary>Reveal</summary>

1. **B** — the repeatable acquisition path. (L1 §1)
2. **B** — high price, few reachable customers. (L1 §2)
3. **C** — page views with no behind-it behavior. (L2 §2)
4. **B** — the leakiest step moves the whole funnel most. (L2 §3)
5. **C** — retention is hardest to fake. (L2 §4)

</details>
