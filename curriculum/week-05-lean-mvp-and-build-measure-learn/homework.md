# Week 5 Homework

Three problems plus the build. Work in your Week 5 repo. Keep interviewing toward ten.

---

## Problem 1 — Assumption map

**Problem statement.** At `notes/assumption-map.md`, list every assumption your idea depends on (aim for 8+), score each on uncertainty and fatality, and circle the riskiest.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; 8+ assumptions scored; one circled. Committed.

**Hint.** Adoption and willingness-to-pay are usually riskier than feasibility for non-deep-tech ideas.

**Estimated time.** 30 min.

---

## Problem 2 — MVP build log

**Problem statement.** Build your chosen MVP and keep a log at `notes/mvp-build-log.md`: what you built, the tools used (free/low-cost), how long it took, and the link/screenshot of the artifact.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; artifact linked or screenshotted; time recorded; built in roughly two days, not three weeks. Committed.

**Hint.** Carrd, Google Forms, Figma, or doing it by hand all count. Build less than feels comfortable.

**Estimated time.** ~3 hours (the week's build).

---

## Problem 3 — Result and decision

**Problem statement.** At `notes/mvp-result.md`, record the metric you measured against your pre-set threshold, what you learned, and your persevere/pivot decision with the number that drove it.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; metric vs. threshold stated; an explicit persevere/pivot decision with reasoning. Committed.

**Hint.** A miss is not a failure of the week — it is the week working. Pivot honestly.

**Estimated time.** 45 min.
