# Capstone — Validated Startup Concept

> The whole course converges here. Assemble a public repo that walks a stranger from problem to ask, backed by real evidence, and present a 3-minute pitch at demo day.

**Estimated time:** ~1.75 hours of assembly (plus the deck/recording in homework).

## What you will produce

A single public repo (or a top-level folder in your portfolio repo) containing **all** of the following, cross-linked from a top `README.md`:

1. **Discovery dossier** — ≥10 interview write-ups (no private identifiers) and a synthesized pattern (`discovery-dossier/`, from Week 3, grown).
2. **Lean business model canvas** — nine blocks (`canvas/`, from Week 6).
3. **Unit-economics sheet** — CAC, LTV, contribution margin, payback, with assumptions (`unit-economics/`, from Week 6).
4. **MVP or clickable prototype** — the artifact and its experiment result (`mvp/`, from Week 5; polish optional).
5. **Go-to-market one-pager** — motion, metric, channel-test result (`gtm/`, from Week 7).
6. **Co-founder agreement draft** — roles, equity, vesting (`cofounder/`, from Week 4).
7. **Cap-table + dilution model and raise memo** — (`cap-table/`, from Week 8).
8. **Incorporation & ops checklist** — (`ops/`, from Week 9).
9. **Pitch deck** — `pitch/deck.pdf` (Week 10 homework).
10. **3-minute recorded pitch** — linked in `pitch/RECORDING.md` (Week 10 homework).

The top `README.md` tells the story in order and links to each artifact.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Public repo with a top `README.md` linking every artifact.
- [ ] ≥10 discovery interviews synthesized into a defensible pattern.
- [ ] Lean canvas and unit economics present and internally consistent.
- [ ] MVP/prototype present with its experiment result.
- [ ] GTM one-pager with a real channel-test result.
- [ ] Co-founder agreement draft (roles, equity, vesting with a cliff).
- [ ] Cap-table model with dilution computed.
- [ ] Incorporation & ops checklist.
- [ ] Pitch deck (PDF) and a 3-minute recording (≤3:00).
- [ ] No private identifiers anywhere; no copyrighted text.
- [ ] Committed and public.

## Capstone rubric (matches the syllabus)

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|------:|
| Discovery evidence: ≥10 interviews, honestly synthesized | 25% |
| MVP/prototype tests the stated riskiest assumption, with a result | 20% |
| Lean canvas + unit economics consistent, assumptions stated | 15% |
| Deck + 3-min recording land the structure | 20% |
| Co-founder agreement covers roles, decision rights, equity, vesting+cliff | 10% |
| Pitch survives the three obvious hard questions | 10% |

## Demo day

Present live (or play the recording) to the cohort, take questions, and post your repo URL in the channel. Watch two peers' pitches and leave one specific, kind, evidence-focused comment each.
