# Week 10 Homework

The week's work is the capstone itself. These problems scaffold it. Work in your Week 10 repo.

---

## Problem 1 — Build the deck

**Problem statement.** Build an 8–10 slide deck (Google Slides, reveal.js, or Marp) following the Lecture 1 §3 structure. Export to PDF into `pitch/deck.pdf` and note in `notes/deck-notes.md` which deliverable backs each slide.

**Acceptance criteria.** Deck exported; 8–10 slides; one idea per slide; deck-notes maps slides to deliverables. Committed.

**Hint.** Red-pen every adjective that lacks a number behind it.

**Estimated time.** ~1.5 hr.

---

## Problem 2 — Record the pitch

**Problem statement.** Record a 3-minute pitch (OBS or Loom), edit to time, and add the link to `pitch/RECORDING.md`.

**Acceptance criteria.** Recording linked; runs ≤3:00; audible; ends on the ask. Committed.

**Hint.** Audio quality matters most. Two or three takes beat ten.

**Estimated time.** ~1.5 hr.

---

## Problem 3 — Hard-question prep

**Problem statement.** At `notes/hard-questions.md`, write evidence-based answers to the three obvious hard questions and to "what's your riskiest assumption."

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; four answers, each citing a deliverable or a test plan. Committed.

**Hint.** Treat the questions as free advice, not attacks.

**Estimated time.** 45 min.
