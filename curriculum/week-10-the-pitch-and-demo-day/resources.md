## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Pitch deck** | The ~10-slide story of the company |
| **The ask** | The specific thing you want from the audience |
| **Demo day** | The event where founders present pitches |
| **Evidence-first** | Leading with proof, not adjectives |
| **Riskiest assumption** | The thing most likely to sink it; name it calmly |
