# Week 9 Homework

Three problems, about **2 hours**. Work in your Week 9 repo. Reach ten interviews if you have not yet.

---

## Problem 1 — Trap audit

**Problem statement.** At `notes/trap-audit.md`, honestly audit your venture for the classic traps: code/assets on personal accounts, missing IP assignment, contributors with no agreement, your own prior-employment IP situation. For each: present-state and the fix.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; all four traps checked; fixes noted. Committed.

**Hint.** "We have no contributors yet" is a valid answer — write it down so future-you remembers to assign IP when you do.

**Estimated time.** 40 min.

---

## Problem 2 — Core agreements list

**Problem statement.** At `notes/agreements-list.md`, list the agreements your venture needs now or soon (founders, IP assignment, advisor, contractor/employee, NDA-if-any). For each: needed now or later, and who must sign.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; at least four agreements; timing and signers noted; NDA use justified or set aside. Committed.

**Hint.** Most early conversations do not need an NDA. Note where one is genuinely warranted.

**Estimated time.** 40 min.

---

## Problem 3 — Ops minimum plan

**Problem statement.** At `notes/ops-minimum.md`, write your operational minimum: bank account, bookkeeping approach, document storage, and a compliance/deadline note. Keep it short and un-over-engineered.

**Acceptance criteria.** File present; all four ops items; deliberately minimal. Committed.

**Hint.** If your plan needs ten tools, it is too much for this stage.

**Estimated time.** 40 min.
