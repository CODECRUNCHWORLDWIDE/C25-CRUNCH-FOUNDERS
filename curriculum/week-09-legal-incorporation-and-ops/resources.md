## Glossary

| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Incorporation** | Forming a legal company entity |
| **Entity type** | The legal form of the company (jurisdiction-specific) |
| **Founder stock** | Shares issued to founders, ideally subject to vesting |
| **IP assignment** | Agreement that work belongs to the company |
| **Founders' agreement** | The papered version of your Week 4 terms |
| **Commingling** | Mixing personal and company money — avoid it |
| **Compliance calendar** | A list of filing/tax deadlines |
