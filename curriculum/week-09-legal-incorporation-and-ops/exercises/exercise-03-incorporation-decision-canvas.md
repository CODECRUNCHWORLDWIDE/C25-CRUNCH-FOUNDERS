# Exercise 3 — Incorporation Decision Canvas (Template)

> This is a **one-page fill-in template**, not code. Copy it into your Week 9 repo as
> `exercises/exercise-03-incorporation-decision-canvas.md`, replace every `<…>` and `TODO`,
> and commit it. It's the single page you'd hand a lawyer so they can give you a real entity
> recommendation in minutes instead of interviewing you for an hour.

**Goal:** Capture, on one page, the facts that determine your incorporation decision, the candidate entity options with their trade-offs, and the open questions only a professional can close.

**Estimated time:** 45 minutes.

> **This is education, not legal or tax advice.** The canvas captures your *situation and options*; the *decision* on a non-trivial or cross-border setup is made with a professional. Mark those items `[NEEDS ADVISOR]`.

## Hints

- **Fundraising intent dominates.** If you'll raise venture money, that pushes you toward the form investors expect, even at higher overhead. If you won't, simpler is usually better. Be honest about which you are.
- **Don't resolve the cross-border question yourself.** Two founders in two countries is a genuine lawyer question; capture it as `[NEEDS ADVISOR]` and move on.
- **The canvas is a hypothesis.** You're not deciding the entity here — you're making the lawyer's job a five-minute confirmation instead of a one-hour interview.
