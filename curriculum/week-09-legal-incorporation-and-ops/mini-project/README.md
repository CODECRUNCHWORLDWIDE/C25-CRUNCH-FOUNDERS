# Mini-Project — Incorporation & Ops Checklist

> Produce a tailored incorporation and operations checklist for your concept, with every jurisdiction- or situation-specific item flagged for a real advisor. It demonstrates you know what a clean company setup requires and where you need professional help.

**This is education, not legal or tax advice.**

**Estimated time:** ~1.75 hours.

## What you will produce

A file `ops/INCORPORATION-OPS-CHECKLIST.md` with:

### 1. Incorporation steps
The sequence for your situation (form entity → issue founder stock with vesting → IP assignment → bank account → bookkeeping → records → deadlines), each marked **DIY** or **needs advisor**.

### 2. Agreements register
A table of the agreements you need, who signs each, and status (have / need / later).

### 3. IP & trap fixes
The output of your trap audit: each classic trap, present-state, and the fix.

### 4. Ops minimum
Bank account, bookkeeping approach, document storage, compliance/deadline note — deliberately minimal.

### 5. Advisor questions
The lawyer and accountant question lists from Exercise 1.

### 6. Not-advice note
A visible statement that this is a planning checklist, not legal or tax advice, and that binding/jurisdiction-specific items go to professionals.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] File at `ops/INCORPORATION-OPS-CHECKLIST.md`.
- [ ] Incorporation steps, each marked DIY or advisor.
- [ ] Agreements register with signers and status.
- [ ] IP/trap fixes from the audit.
- [ ] A minimal ops section.
- [ ] Advisor question lists included.
- [ ] A "not legal/tax advice" note.
- [ ] Committed.

## Rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|------:|
| Steps are sequenced and correctly flagged DIY vs. advisor | 30% |
| Agreements register is complete with signers | 25% |
| IP/trap audit is honest and has fixes | 25% |
| Ops minimum is genuinely minimal | 10% |
| Advisor questions are specific to the venture | 10% |
